About 26 January

Republic Day in India is celebrated on 26th January every year to honor the date on which the Constitution of India came into effect in 1950, making India a sovereign republic. It is a national holiday marked by grand parades, cultural performances, and patriotic displays across the country.

Facts About Republic Day

  • Republic Day is celebrated on 26 January every year.
  • The Constitution of India came into effect in 1950.
  • The main celebration is held in New Delhi.
  • A grand parade is conducted at Kartavya Path.
  • It is one of the three national holidays of India.
